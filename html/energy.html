Energy

    Energy production accounts for over 25% of greenhouse gas emissions and is a major contributor to global warming. Energy production relies heavily on fossil fuels like coal and oil. As more and more countries become industrialized, they will use more coal and oil as a cheap and reliable source of energy instead of clean energy.

Tips/Tricks

    • Install solar panels
    • Use energy efficient appliances with ratings of B or higher
    • Turn off lights when not in use
    • Only run the dishwasher or washing machine when full
    • Don’t leave appliances plugged in when you go on vacation or won’t be using them for a while
