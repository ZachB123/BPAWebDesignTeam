Why Care?

  Why should we care about reducing our carbon footprints? Carbon causes the greenhouse effect where the sun's energy gets trapped inside the atmosphere thereby increasing global tempuratures. Increased global tempuratures have consquences everywhere on the planet from rising sea levels to extinction of species and more severe weather.

Temperature Over Time

  Carbon trapped in the atmosphere makes it harder for the sun's energy to escape which causes global temperatures to increase. This increase of tempurature over time is know as global warming.

Sea Levels Over Time

  The chart shows how sea levels have been steadily rising since 1993. The water comes from the polar ice caps that are melting. Increasing water levels could completely wipe out many small islands.

Recorded Tornadoes in U.S

  Tornadoes are one of the many consequences of rising tempuratures. Tornadoes can be extremely deadly and cause millions of dollars in damages. Reducing your carbon footprint could save lives.
